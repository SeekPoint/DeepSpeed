https://blog.csdn.net/Annie20180423/article/details/108020703

pytest中print的坑
pytest确实不会输出print的信息到控制台，如果要想打印所有内容，需要运行时加上"-s"参数，即pytest -s xxx.py。
ex
(ds_py10) amd00@MZ32-00:~/yk_repo/ds/DeepSpeed/tests/accelerator$ pytest -s test_ds_init.py > test_ds_init.log
Using /home/amd00/.cache/torch_extensions/py310_cu121 as PyTorch extensions root...
